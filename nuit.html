An App To End Your Day With

So, you're a UX/UI designer, but where are your UX/UI design projects?

I realized that I really didn't have that many UX/UI design projects on my portfolio, and so I challenged myself to come up with an app idea, design/animate it, and document the project all within one week. This is by no means a "finished" design, but I'm pretty happy with what I was able to do in such a short amount of time!

An App To End Your Day With

Let's be honest, most of us are guilty of scrolling through social media before bed despite knowing that we probably shouldn't. The goal of this is app isn't to ask people not to, but to provide a better way to do so before going to sleep.

Designed To Be Used With the Brightness Down Low

Nuit is designed for night-time use. The app features a dark UI, minimal design, and simple interactions. Users can check tomorrows to-do list, quickly jot down any late night ideas they have, and scroll through their Twitter feed without being blasted with bright colours. And yes, there is a stopping cue.
