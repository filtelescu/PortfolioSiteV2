3D Work

A showcase of some of my recent Renders!

Since my first year of university I became super interested in the AR/VR space, playing around with various VR headsets and even trying the Focals by North AR glasses at an AR event in Toronto. While I observed the space for a while, it wasn't until August 2021 when I decided I wanted to learn to create my own worlds and experiences.

My first goal was to learn 3D in order to model my own objects & environments, so I set off to learn Blender with the renowned donut tutorial by Blender Guru. Since then I've been continuing to practice 3D in my free time, and I thought I'd post some of my renders here to keep track of my progress!
