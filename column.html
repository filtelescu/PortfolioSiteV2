Column

The Future of Newsletters

Column is a mobile-first newsletter app that affords users the ability to create, discover, and manage newsletters like never before

I've wanted to improve my UI & presentation skills for some time now, and what better way to do that than by building (or designing) out an app idea I had some time ago? This project was done in a week and I think it really reflects my growth as a designer in the last year.

Reading newsletters is something I really enjoy, but I couldn't help but feel that the whole experience was lagging behind others. Unsubscribing is a hassle, signing up with an email address seems like a thing of the past, and I couldn't help but feel that newsletters have the potential to be much more engaging than they currently are. So I set out to design Column, an app that improves upon today's newsletter experience.
